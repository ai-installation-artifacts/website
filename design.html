DESIGN

Artifacts presents itself with intentional reduction. Like an abandoned office that was forgotten but continues to operate. The environment is clinical, quiet and almost empty. There is no visual overload as the focus is on the interaction.

Colors

The color palette revolves around white, grey, black and the yellowed plastic of outdated office technology. Everything feels slightly old and somewhat deserted. Subtle contrasts emerge between analog and digital, between past and present.

Materials

Materials are chosen for their technical character and functional appearance. Yet nothing remains fully controlled. Shredded paper piles up unpredictably on the floor, like scattered evidence of an invisible process.

Sound

The sound design avoids music or voices. It focuses entirely on mechanical sounds that make every interaction unmistakably present. The space listens.

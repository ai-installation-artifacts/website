ABOUT

Artifacts is an interactive installation that invites visitors to submit personal data in exchange for a seemingly personalized poem.

What begins as a poetic gesture soon becomes a reflection on data, control, and artificial intelligence: From a few simple details, not only a poem is generated, but also documents that push the data beyond its original intent.

These artifacts reveal how personal information can be captured, transformed, and reframed. The original control is slipping quietly out of hand.

A single input gives more than just data. It hands over control. What AI creates from it can’t be undone. Or can it?
